Services we offer

CBSE affiliation is a bigger milestone in any school’s journey. It requires a lot of attention along with documentation procedures. Our team at Adhyapan will guide you through this entire journey and make it easier for schools to get the affiliation.

STAGE-1   Requirement & Audit
Infrastructure, Licences, Documentation Audit                                                       
STAGE-2   Compliance
Online Application Review and Compliance Check                                                       
STAGE-3   Gap Analysis
Training the teachers and other staff members                                                       
STAGE-4   CBSE Inspection
Coordinating with the school for CBSE Inspection team and Closure Report
